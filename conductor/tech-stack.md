# Technology Stack: Sambodhi Retreat Centre

## 1. Core Frameworks
- **Frontend & App Framework:** [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org) (App Router)
- **UI Library:** [React 19](https://react.dev)
- **CMS & Backend:** [Payload CMS 3.x](https://payloadcms.com)

## 2. Languages & Tooling
- **Primary Language:** [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org)
- **Package Manager:** [pnpm](https://pnpm.io)

## 3. Styling & UI
- **Styling Framework:** [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com)
- **UI Components:** [Shadcn UI](https://ui.shadcn.com)
- **Animations:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)

## 4. Data & Storage
- **Local Database:** [SQLite](https://sqlite.org) (via @payloadcms/db-sqlite)
- **Media Storage:** Local filesystem / S3-compatible (configurable)

## 5. Testing & Quality
- **End-to-End Testing:** [Playwright](https://playwright.dev)
- **Integration & Unit Testing:** [Vitest](https://vitest.dev)
- **Linting:** ESLint
- **Formatting:** Prettier
