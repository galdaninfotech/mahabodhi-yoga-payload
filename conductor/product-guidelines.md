# Product Guidelines: Sambodhi Retreat Centre

## 1. Voice & Tone
- **Serene & Encouraging:** Our prose should be peaceful and supportive, creating a welcoming atmosphere for all practitioners.
- **Spiritual & Wise:** We speak with depth and contemplation, honoring the wisdom of yoga traditions without being exclusionary.
- **Accessible & Clear:** We prioritize clarity, ensuring that even complex spiritual or technical concepts are understandable to beginners.

## 2. Brand Values
- **Authenticity:** We remain grounded in the true roots of yoga and spiritual practice.
- **Inclusivity:** Every aspect of our digital presence must feel welcoming to individuals of all backgrounds and experience levels.
- **Tranquility:** The user experience should be a digital extension of the calm found in a yoga studioâ€”minimalist, focused, and uncluttered.

## 3. Content & Media Standards
- **Clarity over Complexity:** Use language that is accessible yet profound. Avoid jargon unless explained or used in a way that adds depth.
- **Visual Harmony:** Use high-quality, serene imagery that supports the text and enhances the overall sense of tranquility.
- **Supportive Instructions:** When providing instructions or technical details, use clear formatting and visual aids to guide the user effectively.
