# Initial Concept\nA production-ready yoga website built using Payload CMS and Next.js.

## 1. Vision & Purpose
Mahabodhi Yoga is a comprehensive digital platform designed to serve as the heart of a vibrant yoga community. It seamlessly integrates a production-ready website with a powerful Content Management System (Payload CMS) to inspire spiritual growth, manage yoga education, and foster community engagement.

## 2. Target Users
- **New Students:** Seekers looking for yoga classes, schedules, and an introduction to the community's offerings.
- **Existing Members:** Dedicated practitioners managing their bookings, subscriptions, and ongoing engagement with the studio.
- **Spiritual Seekers:** Individuals interested in deep-diving into retreats, spiritual insights, and community-driven success stories.

## 3. Core Goals
- **Drive Engagement:** Increase class attendance and retreat bookings through an intuitive, dynamic scheduling system.
- **Foster Community:** Build a strong sense of belonging by sharing success stories, yoga philosophy, and spiritual insights.
- **Streamline Operations:** Automate administrative tasks such as member management and newsletter distribution to focus more on spiritual and educational delivery.

## 4. Key Features
- **Integrated Scheduling & Booking:** A dynamic system for managing classes and retreats, fully integrated with Payload CMS for easy updates.
- **Newsletter & Communication:** A robust subscriber management and newsletter system for sharing spiritual updates and event notifications.
- **Content Hub:** A rich repository of testimonials, blog posts, and educational content focusing on yoga philosophy and spiritual practice.
